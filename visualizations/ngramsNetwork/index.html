Co-occurrence and sentiment network

Articles relating to Ukraine from Western and Russian State Media

Sliding window size: . For example, words s and t have a co-occurrence of 100 if they occur together in at 100 sliding windows. Lowering the co-occurrence threshold to c will disappear those links with less than c co-occurrences. The color of a link is based on the average polarity of sentences containing a co-occurrence window with words s and t (using the rule-based VADER sentiment analyzer). A polarity of -1 is the most negative, while a polarity of +1 is the most positive. A polarity of 0 is neutral. Polarity values get to be quite small after averaging over >100 sentences.
